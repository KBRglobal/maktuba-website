# Design Notes

## Tinder Reference
- Single viewport hero with full-screen background image
- Big bold tagline centered
- Single CTA button
- Minimal nav: logo + few links
- Footer with legal links, app store badges
- Extremely simple - one screen, one message, one action

## MAKTUBA One-Pager Plan
- Hero: Full viewport, dark bg with candle/tarot image, MAKTUBA wordmark, tagline, download CTA
- Brief "what is it" section with 3 icons
- Phone mockup section
- Final CTA with app store badges
- Footer with legal links (privacy, terms, support, delete-data)
- Keep it SIMPLE and ELEGANT
